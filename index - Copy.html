About Us

We, at Take Your Medicine Innovations (TYMI), will create a medication adherence monitoring device that reminds patients to take their medications on time and aids healthcare professionals (HCPs) in accurately assessing treatment based on medication adherence performance. To achieve this goal, we will commit to consistent, thorough documentation of our end-to-end design process and deliver a validated, effective, and user-friendly prototype to the industry sponsor upon the completion of BMEN 454.

The Problem

Medication nonadherence is a widespread and persistent public health concern that affects 50% of patients who are prescribed chronic medications, and 31% of patients fail to fill their first prescriptions. This causes 125,000 preventable deaths each year and accounts for 10-70% of medication-related readmissions. The economic impact is alarming, with estimated costs of $100-$500 billion annually. Medication non-adherence diminishes the effectiveness of treatment, exacerbates disease progression, and reduces patient quality of life. Current solutions such as patient education, regimen changes, counseling, and follow-ups lack standardized monitoring and real-time feedback mechanisms. Ultimately, behavioral and technical constraints exist that leave healthcare professionals without the reliable tools to properly manage in real time patient medication adherence that can seamlessly fit into patient lives.
